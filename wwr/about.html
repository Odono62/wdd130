Rapids White Water Rafting

Our goal is to provide you with the best experience possible. We have been providing unforgettable experiences for over 30 years. We are a family owned and operated business dedicated to giving you the adventure of a lifetime.

History

Rapids started floating the river in 1995 with just two rafts and a dream. What began as a small family operation has grown into one of the most respected rafting companies in the region.

For over 25 years, we've been providing unforgettable experiences. Our team of experienced guides is dedicated to ensuring your safety while delivering the thrill of a lifetime on the rapids.
